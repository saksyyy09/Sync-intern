Project E-learning Website
Description
The E-learning Website is an online platform designed to provide interactive educational content to users. It aims to facilitate learning in a flexible and accessible manner, allowing users to access courses, lectures, quizzes, and other educational resources remotely.

Features
User Registration: Users can create an account to access the platform's features and personalize their learning experience.
Course Catalog: A comprehensive catalog of courses is available, covering various subjects and levels of difficulty.
Course Enrollment: Users can enroll in courses of their choice and track their progress.
Video Lectures: Courses include video lectures that users can watch at their own pace.
Quizzes and Assessments: Interactive quizzes and assessments are provided to evaluate users' understanding and progress.
Discussion Forums: Users can participate in discussion forums to interact with peers and instructors, ask questions, and share insights.
Progress Tracking: The platform keeps track of users' progress within each course, allowing them to monitor their learning journey.
Certification: Upon successful completion of a course, users can receive certificates to showcase their achievements.
